# Contributing

> [!NOTE]
> Use this guideline to the pict-wasm specific WebAssembly, JavaScript, or other supplementary components.

Thank you for your interest in this project!

## Current status

At this time, this project is primarily maintained by the author,
and we are **not actively seeking external contributions**.

This may change in the future as the project matures.

## Issues

- Bug reports and questions are welcome.
- Please note that response time may be limited.

## Pull Requests

- We are currently not accepting general feature PRs.
- Small fixes (typos, broken links, obvious bugs) may be considered.
- Please open an issue first if you want to discuss a change.

Thank you for your understanding.
